    Thar

    The Mahindra Thar is a rugged and iconic off-road SUV manufactured by the Indian automaker Mahindra & Mahindra. It is known for its robust design, excellent off-road capabilities, and a history that dates back to its initial launch in 1947. The Thar has been a popular choice among off-road enthusiasts and adventure seekers in India and other select markets.
